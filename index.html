Dr. Attiq ur Rehman

Plant Geneticist & Quantitative Breeder

I am a plant scientist specializing in genomics-assisted pre-breeding, quantitative genetics, and image-based phenotyping. My post-doctoral research at the Natural Resources Institute Finland (Luke) focuses on learning and developing advanced genomic prediction methods in crop plants, exploring reaction norm models in MiX99, and integrating artificial intelligence approaches across multi-omics datasets.

My doctoral work at the University of Helsinki was highly centered on developing climate-resilient Fragaria × ananassa (octoploid garden strawberry) germplasm for Northern Europe. This involved carrying out genome-wide association studies (GWAS), QTL mapping, population structure analysis, and high-throughput phenotyping workflows.
